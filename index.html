Empowering Creativity With Art + Literature

Finding beautiful words is a uniquely personal journey, a path we must take on our own.

BeautifulWords.Org's mission is to provide a platform for creative engagement, which empowers youth, and our community, to find their own words. We believe that fostering creativity, innovation, and awareness of different perspectives is vital in a changing world.

Drawing from rich themes, our initiatives are designed to bring the learning process to life – helping participants gain confidence in their ability to pursue knowledge, reflect, respond, create, and dream.

With Your Support

Supporting The Arts

Showcasing Work

We believe that supporting the arts in our community is important. Beautifulwords.org offers the opportunity for guests to explore topics together.

Through written word, experiential art, performance pieces, and media; BeautifulWords.Org themes come to life. Local Artisans, Small Businesses, Performers, and Artists, showcase their work engaging raising awareness, and forming connections with their community.

**Charitable co-sponsored events donate a portion of the proceeds to a 501c3 selected by the event co-host.

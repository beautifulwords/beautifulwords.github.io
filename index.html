Supporting The Arts

Local Artists + Local Dialogue

We believe that supporting artists in our community is important. Whether we bring out the artist in you, or help bring text2life's dialogue to life. Beautifulwords.org offers the opportunity for guests to experience text2life's themes, and contribute to the conversation.

At our events, local artisans, Performers, Painters, and Volunteers bring their talents center stage. With dance, experiential art, installations, performance pieces, speakers, and film, text2life themes come alive.

Proceeds from these events help fund book giveaways, and provide resources for future community events.

**Fundraising events donate a portion of the profits to a 501c3 selected by the event host.
